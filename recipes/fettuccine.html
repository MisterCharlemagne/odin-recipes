Delicious Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

This recipe will teach you how to create a staple Italian dish, beloved by all: Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Butter: 2 sticks
  • 1 cup of heravy cream
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic salt
  • Romano and Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Once boiling, cook pasta for 15-20 minutes or until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, melt butter and the cream together in a large pan. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic salt. bring to a simmer and then add cheese until melted.
  3. Once pasta is finished, drain and combine with sauce in pan. Toss pasta until coated in sauce, then divide between plates or bowls.
  4. Garish your Fettuccine Alfredo with whatever you like. I like to use cilantro. Serve and enjoy!
